How to Play

1. Click piece → Yellow ring

2. Click in ring → Move

3. Tab key cycles available Hunters

4. Arrow keys move selected Hunter

5. Enter confirms move

6. Hunters dim after moving (✓)

7. ROAR! Red pulse = Tiger threatens

8. Camp warning Orange ring = 2 turns in Borderlands

9. Camp penalty 3 turns in Borderlands = removal

10. Hunter Types: S=Scout, V=Veteran, M=Medic

11. Tiger Range: Scales with size × multiplier
